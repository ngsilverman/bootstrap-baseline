{LESS} Baseline

The baseline is the line upon which letters "sit".

You can see on this page every line of text and every title conforms to a vertical grid. Everything sits neatly on a line. Now, the print industry—books, newspapers, magazines—have been doing this forever because it's best for the readers. It's easier to read, it simply feels right.

And yet, nearly every website you've ever visited breaks this rule. Not that they mean to. It's just that CSS makes it a hassle to conform to the same baseline accross an entire page. Enter Baseline.
